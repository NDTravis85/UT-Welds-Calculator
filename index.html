UT Field Tools

Inspector-focused ultrasonic field calculators

Calculators

Scan Distance

Automatically adds 0.5 in HAZ to each side of the weld and returns the minimum scan distance from weld centerline required for full opposite-side HAZ coverage.

% of DAC

Enter the value exactly as shown on the equipment. A positive number entered on the instrument is treated as a negative exponent in the %DAC formula.

Near Field

Uses the flat circular element approximation:
Near Field = (D² × f) / (4V)

Trajectory

Assumes straight planar geometry. Reports estimated indication location as a surface measurement from the end of the wedge, along with leg and skip information.

Weld Progress

Assumes circumferential weld travel at a maximum of 6 in/s. The tool also reports the maximum indexed advance per pass using 50% overlap of the entered transducer width. Time values are ideal travel times and do not include setup, coupling, or repositioning.
