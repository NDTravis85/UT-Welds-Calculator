Inspector-focused ultrasonic field calculators

Calculators

Built for quick field use with large touch targets, dark mode, and simple navigation.

Scan Distance

Automatically adds 0.5 in HAZ to each side of the weld and returns the minimum scan distance from weld centerline required for full opposite-side HAZ coverage.

% of DAC

Enter the value exactly as shown on the equipment. A positive number entered on the instrument is treated as a negative exponent in the %DAC formula.

Near Field

Uses the flat circular element approximation:
Near Field = (D² × f) / (4V)
with D in inches, f in MHz, and V in in/µs. Approximate focal distance beyond the wedge is shown as Near Field minus wedge thickness.
